Jessie Gray Singer is a writer and illustrator from New York City and Senior Editor at Transportation Alternatives. She is the Editor in Chief of Reclaim magazine and the journal Vision Zero Cities.

Her work as has been published in New York Magazine, The Awl, The Guardian, and many more. Follow her work about accountability here on TinyLetter.
