Jessie Singer is an activist and investigative journalist whose writing appears in BuzzFeed, The Awl, New York Magazine, The Guardian, and elsewhere. She is an award-winning editor, and currently serves as senior editor at Transportation Alternatives, the editor in chief of Reclaim Magazine, and the founding editor of the international Vision Zero Cities Journal. She is a graduate of the Arthur L. Carter School of Journalism at New York University.

Jessie was born and lives in New York City. She is represented by Stephen Barr at Writers House.
