Accident

In 2015, Jessie launched the #crashnotaccident hashtag campaign to change how media sources reports on traffic crashes. Since that time, her campaign has been featured on the front page of the New York Times, and covered by outlets from Wired to the Boston Globe. Jessie now writes about all varieties of American accidents, from oil spills to missing bullets to circus falls, in search of answers about what we mean when we say, "it was an accident."

Reclaim Magazine

With an audience of 12,000 print subscribers and 10,000 online readers, Reclaim Magazine is the voice of #BikeNYC. Jessie is the editor in chief and lead writer for the biannual magazine, and regularly sits down with New York City officials and cycling heroes to give readers an inside look at biking in New York. ​Send pitches for Reclaim Magazine to Jessie at (at) transalt (dot) org.
